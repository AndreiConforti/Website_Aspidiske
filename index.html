About me

Aspidiske's sound is unique and striking, with grooves that are sure to please.

Aspidiske is an electronic music producer, with a very unique sound that mixes Trance and House elements and striking grooves. Tracks are often 135 to 145 bpm and 6 to 12 (usually about 7 to 8) minutes and start with about 30 seconds of atmospheric sounds, while the main part of the track is split into two halves, sometimes with the bottom of tracks changed; most are stacked at the end of both halves, there is usually a transition or interlude between the two halves.
